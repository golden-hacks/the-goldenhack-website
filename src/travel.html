Travel Info

# Parking

Parking is free on weekends from 7AM - 11PM in the Lazaridis Hall Parking Lot (see the map to the right)

During the night, you’re going to want to park on a nearby street and fill out an overnight parking permit. Make sure you do this so you don’t get a parking ticket!!

# Transit

If you're travelling from other cities in the vicinity of Waterloo, we recommend these options:

Downtown Toronto: GO Bus from Union Station
London: ViaRail to Kitchener, and then bus to Waterloo
Mississauga: GO Bus from Square One
Hamilton: GO Bus from McMaster
Guelph: GO Bus from the University of Guelph
