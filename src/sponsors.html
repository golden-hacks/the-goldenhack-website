Sponsor The GoldenHack

Dear Potential Sponsor,

I am delighted to invite you to participate in a exclusive opportunity with The GoldenHack. Together, we can ensure that awareness about your company is spread to exceptional students interested in design, technology and business. Our organization, The GoldenHack, was founded on the values of teamwork, the entrepreneurial spirit and educational growth to help bridge the divide between technology, business and design.

As a business hackathon, our aim is to blend the worlds of business and technology. We often find that business and design students are not encouraged to participate or are underrepresented in hackathons and we want to change that! Wilfrid Laurier University prides itself on its business, computer science and user experience design programs, so we decided that it was time to bring these complementary disciplines together in The GoldenHack.

Our hackathon will attract hundreds of students to come compete at the Lazaridis School of Business and Economics and will provide them, and you, with opportunities to network, learn, solve problems in new and different ways. Our goal is to show the world that innovation is a team sport. We’d love to be the event where a designer, a developer and a business student decide to co-found the next massive social network. Or maybe the next great fintech solution. We will create an atmosphere where the entrepreneurial and creative spirit thrives, and where everyone feels valued.

This October 5-6 will represent our inaugural year as The GoldenHack, and your company will be displayed to all students at the event and the broader community at Wilfrid Laurier University and in Waterloo. We hope that you believe in this just as much as we do and can see the value we offer you and the community we serve. We’d love this to commence long term relationships that continue to be beneficial to everyone involved.

Thank you for your consideration.

Sincerely,
Natasha Kasunic
President, The GoldenHack
